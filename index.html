About Me

I am currently a PhD student in the School of Electronic Science and Engineering at Nanjing University, supervised by Yang Gao, Hongbing Pan, and Wenbing Li. I am a member of the Reasoning and Learning Research Group, led by Professor Yang Gao. Previously, I interned for a year at the National Institute of Healthcare Data Science at Nanjing University before working as a Research Assistant in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at the same university. I received my master's degree from Minnan Normal University in 2023, supervised by Hong Zhao.
