About Me

I am Zhiping Wu. I am currently a PhD student in the School of Electronic Science and Engineering at Nanjing University, supervised by Yang Gao, Hongbing Pan, and Wenbing Li. I am a member of the Reasoning and Learning Research Group, led by Professor Yang Gao. Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University.
